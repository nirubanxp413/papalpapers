Benedict XVI, a former Prefect of the Congregation for the Doctrine of the Faith (CDF), utilized this document to address internal "confusion" regarding the *missio ad gentes* (mission to the nations) in the face of rising religious pluralism and the perceived decline of traditional missionary zeal in the West.

It attempts to set a normative framework for ecumenical dialogue, warning against "indifferentism."
